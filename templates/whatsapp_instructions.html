Send Shopping List or Recipe through WhatsApp

1. You will get a contact card with the active number of whin, and you need to add the card to the contacts on your phone.

2. Send a WhatsApp message from your phone (not from WhatsApp web) to the number you just added on #1. The content of the message must be the contact card you just added to your phone. Make sure you send whin's contact card, any other message will be ignored.

3. The bot will return a 5 digits code as a response to your WhatsApp. You have 30 minutes to POST the code here. If you run out of time, you can request as many codes as you need (just repeat #2).
